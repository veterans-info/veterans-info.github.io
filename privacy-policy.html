Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

Introduction

Veterans' Preference Guide ("we," "our," or "website") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at veterans-info.github.io.

This website is an informational resource and does not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it.

Information We Collect

Information Automatically Collected

When you visit our website, certain information may be automatically collected through GitHub Pages hosting:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring website
  • Pages visited
  • Date and time of visit

Information You Provide

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Email address (only if you contact us)
  • Any information you include in email communications

Interactive Tool Data

Important: Our interactive eligibility guide tool:

  • Does NOT store any answers you provide
  • Does NOT transmit your responses to any server
  • Processes all information locally in your browser
  • Clears all data when you close the page or restart the tool

How We Use Information

We use automatically collected information to:

  • Understand how visitors use our website
  • Improve website functionality and content
  • Ensure website security and performance
  • Generate aggregate, non-identifying statistics

If you contact us via email, we use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Provide requested information
  • Improve our services based on feedback

Data Storage and Security

  • This website is hosted on GitHub Pages
  • We do not have databases or servers that store user data
  • Email communications are handled through standard email protocols
  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect any information shared with us

Third-Party Services

GitHub Pages

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may collect certain technical information. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for more information.

External Links

Our website contains links to external sites including:

  • Office of Personnel Management (OPM)
  • Department of Veterans Affairs (VA)
  • USAJOBS

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies:

  • Session cookies for basic functionality (automatically deleted when you close your browser)
  • No tracking cookies
  • No advertising cookies
  • No third-party cookies

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access our website without providing personal information
  • Use browser settings to block cookies
  • Request information about any data we may have about you
  • Request correction or deletion of any information you've provided

Do Not Track

Our website respects "Do Not Track" browser settings. When DNT is enabled, we do not track your browsing behavior.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). However, as we do not collect or sell personal information, most CCPA provisions do not apply to our website.

International Users

Our website is intended primarily for users in the United States. If you access our website from outside the U.S., please be aware that any information collected will be processed in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: feedback@veterans-info.github.io
  • Website: veterans-info.github.io

Disclaimer

This is an unofficial informational website. For official determinations regarding Veterans' Preference, please consult with your agency's HR department or a Veterans Service Officer. This privacy policy applies only to veterans-info.github.io and not to any linked external websites.
