Eligibility Overview

This section provides a general overview of the eligibility requirements for Veterans' Preference in federal employment. Detailed information can be found in the official OPM Vet Guide for HR Professionals.

General Requirements

  • Must have served in the U.S. Armed Forces.
  • Must have been separated under honorable conditions.
  • Specific service dates or campaign participation may be required.

For precise eligibility criteria, please refer to the OPM Vet Guide.
